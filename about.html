Founder story

Hi, my name is Rudra.

I built MatchFlow Home after I needed HVAC repair. I posted on Facebook and Nextdoor and got bombarded with dozens of ads and people asking to do the job. That experience pushed me to find a better way, so I built this website to make the process calm, clear, and fast for every homeowner.

MatchFlow uses AI plus manual review by our 7 person team to find the best fit. We look at reviews from hundreds of people and match you with businesses that fit your needs and budget. We share only what providers need to quote the job, and we do not sell your information.

How it works

Fill out the form, upload photos, and submit your request. One of our 7 team members will review it, email you for any quick clarifications, and start matching. If you give us 2-3 days, we will send you three options that fit your budget. Then we connect you by email and phone so you can take it from there. The best part is it is completely free for homeowners.
