Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MatchFlow Home collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit our website or submit a request for services. Please read this policy carefully. By using our website or submitting a request, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Overview

MatchFlow Home helps homeowners connect with local service providers. We collect the information you choose to provide so we can understand your request, share details with businesses that may be a good fit, and connect you if you decide to move forward. We do not sell your information.

This policy applies to information collected through our website, forms, and communications related to your request.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, and ZIP code.
  • Request details: the service you need, urgency, budget range, and description of the issue.
  • Photos and files: any photos or files you upload to help providers understand the job.
  • Communications: messages or emails between you and MatchFlow Home.
  • Technical data: basic device, browser, and usage data for site performance and security.

You are not required to provide photos, but they can help providers estimate more accurately.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Understand your request and prepare a clear summary for providers.
  • Share request details with businesses that may be a fit for your job.
  • Contact you with questions or to clarify details.
  • Send you options and connect you with a provider if you choose to move forward.
  • Improve our website, services, and support experience.
  • Prevent fraud, misuse, or security issues.

We do not use your information to sell advertising and we do not sell your information to third parties.

4. How We Share Information

We share your information only to fulfill your request. Specifically:

  • Request details and photos: We share details and photos with local businesses so they can determine whether they can take the job and provide a quote range.
  • Contact details: We share your contact information only after a business confirms interest and you confirm you want to move forward.
  • Service providers: We use trusted tools (like form and file platforms) to collect and transmit your information. These providers process data on our behalf.
  • Legal or safety reasons: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect users and the public from harm.

We never sell your information. We do not allow providers to use your information for unrelated marketing.

5. Your Choices and Controls

You can:

  • Choose not to upload photos or provide optional details.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal or operational limits.
  • Opt out of a connection if you decide not to proceed with any provider.

To exercise these choices, contact us using the information in the Contact section below.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed to process your request, provide support, or comply with legal obligations. If you request deletion, we will remove your information unless we must keep it for legal, security, or operational reasons.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No system is completely secure, but we work to maintain safeguards appropriate to the data we handle.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to help the site function, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use our pages. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information, contact us so we can remove it.

10. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to power parts of the website and request flow. These services have their own privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their policies if you have concerns.

11. International Users

If you access the website from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the U.S. By using the website, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will reflect the most recent update. If changes are significant, we will post a notice on the website.
